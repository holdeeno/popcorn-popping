KERNEL → POPCORN



Everyone knows that popcorn is a tasty snack, usually enjoyed while being entertained. But it is also entertaining all by itself. After watching this video, I was inspired to figure out why exactly popcorn "pops".

Here is what happens when you throw a bag of popcorn in the microwave:
  • The water inside the kernel expands and turns to steam as the kernel heats up
  • Pressure inside the hull builds (to an astonishing 135 pounds per square inch!)
  • The kernel explodes!
  • The hot starch inside the kernel inflates and spills out, becoming as much as 50 times larger than its original size!
